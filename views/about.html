About Austin

Austin was a senior at Chaparral High School in Scottsdale, Arizona where he was known for his outgoing personality, loud Hawaiian shirts and Panama hats. Austin loved animals, was active in Boy Scouts and was a gymnastics instructor. His circle of friends was large and he will be truly missed.

We invite you to visit Austin's Eagle Scout project sundial at: The Audubon Center 313 S. Central Ave. Phoenix, AZ 85040
